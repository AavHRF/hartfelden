Citizenship

Citizenship is the primary method of engagement in Hartfelden. We offer citizenship to any individual who joins our Discord, and wishes to have it.

Residency

Residency is where you only have a nation in our region. Residents often do not have Discord accounts, but some residents simply don't want to go through the additional step of gaining citizenship.
