Diet Procedures Act

A bill for an act to establish procedures for the unicamerial legislative body of the Empire; Creates procedural guidelines for operation of body; Establishes offense of Contempt of Reichstag; Acknowledges Presidential authority.

Judicial Procedures Act

A bill for an act to establish procedures for the judicial body of the Empire; Enforces impartiality; Sets standard of recusal; Informs citizens of their rights; Examines trial structure overall.
